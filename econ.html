About Us

The Econ club, the lone club of Thapar University that looks into worldly matters with economic interest. Started in 2009, the members have made sure to come up with something good and concrete with every passing year, may it be Mock IPL auction for cricket enthusiasts or The Mock Stock Exchange for the Share market lovers. Something new something innovative, EVERYTIME. And this time, it'll get even bigger and better with THE WORLD ECONOMIC FORUM. Other than these high voltage events, some guest lecture's to inspire you about entrepreneurship and to choose the field related to it would act as cherry on the cake. So join us to experience the rich endeavors and enter the whole new world inspired by economics.
